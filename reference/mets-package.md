# mets: Analysis of Multivariate Event Times

Implementation of various statistical models for multivariate event
history data
[doi:10.1007/s10985-013-9244-x](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10985-013-9244-x)
. Including multivariate cumulative incidence models
[doi:10.1002/sim.6016](https://doi.org/10.1002/sim.6016) , and bivariate
random effects probit models (Liability models)
[doi:10.1016/j.csda.2015.01.014](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.csda.2015.01.014)
. Modern methods for survival analysis, including regression modelling
(Cox, Fine-Gray, Ghosh-Lin, Binomial regression) with fast computation
of influence functions.
