# Rates for HPN program for patients of Copenhagen Cohort

Rates for HPN program for patients of Copenhagen Cohort

## Format

crbsi: cumulative rate of catheter related bloodstream infection in HPN
patients of Copenhagen mechanical: cumulative rate of Mechanical
(hole/defect) complication for catheter of HPN patients of Copenhagen
trombo: cumulative rate of Occlusion/Thrombosis complication for
catheter of HPN patients of Copenhagen terminal: rate of terminal event,
patients leaving the HPN program
